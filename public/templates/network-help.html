The workbench provides visual exploration of networks within KBase. Start by selecting one of the example pathways. The genes associated with the pathway are placed in the gray Dock. The Dock is a fixed box that allows you to isolate genes or clusters of interest. A Dock panel is shown at the top right and provides several controls:

  • The Clusters button retrieves all clusters that have the docked genes as their members
  • The Co-Neighbors button is used to retrieve any other members of the clusters that are also connected with the docked genes by another relationship.
  • The Internal button retrieves the internal network among the docked genes, including all connections among them. The internal network is shown in a separate dialog box that for further manipulation such as data export.
